Our Sacred Mission

Official Announcement of the Renovation Works – Shri Vyasaraja Pratishthita Shri Mukhyaprana Devaru (main Prana of God) Temple at the highly sacred place of Koluvarayanahalli.

The renovation includes construction of a new sanctum (Garbha Gudi), Nagabhana (sacred serpent grove), installation of Dwajasthamba (sacred flagpole), building of a front pouli mantapa, roofing, compound wall, stone flooring, and more. Estimated cost: ₹3 Crores.

As the great saying goes: "Dharmo Rakshati Rakshitah" — Dharma protects those who protect it.
